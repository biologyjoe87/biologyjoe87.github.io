I am currently a PhD Candidate in the Graduate Group of Population Biology at the University of California, Davis, working with Sebastian Schreiber. I began at the University of California, Davis in the fall of 2022.

I am broadly interested in using mathematical and computational models to understand phenomena in community, population, and evolutionary ecology.
